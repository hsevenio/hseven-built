What is H7?

H7 is your go to vehicle to ensure your product has quality, visibility and longevity in Web3. We consult, manage, direct and outsource manage projects as if they were our own; leveraging our networks, experience and systems to drive your project to success.

Unlike most agencies who take a ‘one-size fits all’ approach, we offer a service tailored and nurtured project to project. We are bringing together a network of the greatest minds, whales, builders and service providers in the space and allowing them to execute and deliver their visions. When H7 works with a project, we become proactive leaders not passive service providers.

Strategy & Planning

H7 will coordinate meetings and produce documents in partnership with the founders of your business to ensure a coherent strategy for product development, roll-out and longevity.

Team Building

H7 will analyse your project and evaluate the gaps that exist within your core team, filling them accordingly with our network members who provide services of the highest quality renowned in the industry.

Service Delivery

After our core team has produced a clear strategy and timeline, we will determine the best time to release your product and begin campaigns ready to be executed by our most trusted partners.

Outsource Management

When we have established what is needed from our network, we will take full responsibility and ownership of outsource management. From quoting, to KPI monitoring and feedback, H7 will take care of the whole process.

Proactivity & Adaptation

As a boutique agency we understand that every project is different and subject to market conditions in Web3. Many agencies use this as an excuse for poor performance, at H7 this is something we take full accountability for and consider when we work with a project.

Longevity and Client Selection

We are very selective about projects we work with and work with very few projects concurrently. As a result all potential projects must apply via our application form.
