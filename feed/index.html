But only a few people know what spyware is, what it does, and why cybercriminals need it. We will give you a short digression into the concept of spyware. 

What is Spy software? 

This name refers to a whole range of malicious software that can penetrate your laptop, computer, or smartphone to steal data stored on the device. 

What information does Spyware collect? 

The spyware often collects your browsing data: your browsing history, your logins and passwords, and personal data that you enter on the Internet. They can also reveal the data of your payment instruments and collect information about your habits – all these spyware transfers to its owner. Yep, you fully get it: this information will not be used in your favour. 

Can I catch spyware on a mobile gadget? 

Of course, there are many specially adapted spyware for mobile devices. You may not notice their presence because all spyware usually runs in the background. 

How can I infect my device with spyware? 

There are many options. You may accidentally click on an untrusted link or open a spam e-mail in your e-mail. And now, the spyware is already loaded. Often such software is already integrated into unlicensed copies of the software. You can easily download them along with any files from the Internet. Sometimes spyware disguises itself as harmless or even useful utilities available for free on the Internet. So there are a lot of options on how to pick it up. 

Be vigilant, do not download dubious files, do not open e-mails from unknown recipients, and do not follow links leading to suspicious sites. And, of course, don’t forget to use the anti-spyware tools that we offer you.

Tip #1: Use protected cloud storage

Cloud infrastructure is especially relevant for small businesses because it allows you to do without servers and the cost of maintaining them. Secure cloud storage will allow you to operate data without problems while setting access levels and not worrying about protection.

Tip #2: Using e-mail protection is a must
